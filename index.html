Crystal Collector

Crystal Collector is a game of skill that requires quick thinking. At the beginning of the game, a random number is chosen by the computer. You job is to match that number by clicking on the crystals. Each crystal is given a number, each time you click on a crystal it adds a number to your total. You win by clicking on the right combination of cystals to match the the target number chosen by the computer.
