Crystal Collector is a game of skill that requires a nimble mind. At the beginning of the game, a random number is chosen by the computer. Your job is to match that number by clicking on the crystals. Each crystal is given a secret, random number. Each time you click on a crystal, it adds the secret number to your total. You win by clicking on the right combination of cystals to match the the target number chosen by the computer. Play as often as you like. The game will keep track of your Wins and Losses.
